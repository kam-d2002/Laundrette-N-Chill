Amenities
- What we offer.

"Laundrette and Chill" is a new hipster laundrette, a stylish, multi use space located right outside the heart of Bristol city centre, with the aim of creating a more inviting atmosphere than your average cafe. We offer many things, come in for a coffee, some cake and relax, browse our library and have a read, use our laundrette (you can even book a washing machine online!) or come here on one of our music/ open mic nights to let your hair down and have a drink! We offer our building as a community meeting space. Fancy holding an event or party here, we offer competative rates and good quality lighting and sound! Intrigued? Our social media/email links can be found at the bottom of the page. Or... Come down and say hi! We are always happy to answer any queries!
